About the Studio

Respawn Entertainment is an American video game studio founded in 2010 by Vince Zampella and Jason West, creators of the Call of Duty series. The studio is known for the Titanfall and Apex Legends franchises, and since 2019, for the Star Wars Jedi series.

Jedi: Fallen Order

Under the direction of game director Stig Asmussen, the Respawn team created a single-player adventure that blends souls-like combat with metroidvania-style exploration, set in the Star Wars universe.
