Jedi: Fallen Order

The Story

Five years after Order 66 — Cal Kestis's path to rebuilding the Jedi Order.

← Back to Home

A Galaxy in Ruins

After the fall of the Republic and the extermination of the Jedi during Order 66, a surviving Padawan, Cal Kestis, hides in plain sight — working as a mechanic at a scrapping yard on the planet Bracca. When his connection to the Force is accidentally revealed, the Empire dispatches Inquisitors to hunt him down.

A New Hope for the Order

Alongside former Jedi Knight Cere Junda, pilot Greez Dritus, and the droid BD-1, Cal sets out on a journey across the galaxy to find a holocron containing a list of Force-sensitive children — a relic that could help rebuild the shattered Jedi Order.

The Cast

Cal Kestis

A former Padawan who survived Order 66 and rediscovers the Jedi within himself.

Cere Junda

A former Jedi Knight and Cal's mentor, hiding dark secrets of her own.

BD-1

A small astromech droid and Cal's loyal companion throughout the journey.
