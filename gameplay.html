Gameplay

Combat, ancient-world exploration, and growing into a true Jedi.

← Back to Home

Combat System

Combat is built around the lightsaber: blocks, parries, dodges, and precise strikes. Enemies push you to learn their timing and read their attacks, while Force powers — Push, Pull, and Slow — open up new tactical options.

Exploration

The game is built like a metroidvania: levels are interconnected, and new abilities open paths back to areas you couldn't reach before. Secrets, meditation points, and hidden chests reward careful exploration of every planet.
